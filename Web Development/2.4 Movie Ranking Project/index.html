Three Best Movie According to Borno

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Dark Knight

When a menace known as the Joker wreaks havoc and chaos on the people of Gotham, Batman, James Gordon and Harvey Dent must work together to put an end to the madness.

John Wick

John Wick is a former hitman grieving the loss of his true love. When his home is broken into, robbed, and his dog killed, he is forced to return to action to exact revenge.

Mad Unicorn

Giving up his street life, a young man breaks new ground with the launch of a startup business that brings new opportunities and powerful enemies.
